Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-01-15

Overview

Fare Scout provides flight price comparisons and redirects you to booking partners to complete reservations. We do not sell tickets directly and we do not process payments.

Information we collect

  • Contact details such as email address and optional company name.
  • Form responses like travel use case or messages you submit.
  • Basic usage data through analytics and cookies.

How we use information

  • Respond to requests for early access or support.
  • Send product updates when you opt in.
  • Improve product performance and content with analytics.

Analytics and cookies

We use cookies and analytics tools to understand site usage and improve performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Some features may not work without cookies.

Partner redirects and affiliate disclosure

Fare Scout compares prices and may earn a commission when you book through a partner link. When you click "Book", you are redirected to a partner site to complete your purchase.

Payments and ticketing

Fare Scout does not sell tickets, does not process payments, and does not store payment information. All transactions occur on partner sites.

Data retention

We keep personal data only as long as needed to provide the service or meet legal obligations.

Your choices

You can opt out of marketing emails at any time using the link in our emails. You can also contact us to update or delete your information.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at farescout@lemaitre.guru.
