FAQ

Is Fare Scout free?

We may earn a commission from partners when you book; it helps keep the service running.

Do you sell tickets?

No. Fare Scout redirects you to booking partners to complete purchases.

Are prices final?

Final pricing, availability, and policies are confirmed on the partner site.

Which regions are supported?

We are focused on Europe first, with global expansion planned.

Can I set price alerts?

Yes. You can opt in to email alerts for routes and date ranges you track.

Do you store payment info?

No. Payments are handled by booking partners, not Fare Scout.

How do you choose partners?

We evaluate partner coverage, pricing transparency, and user trust before integrating.
